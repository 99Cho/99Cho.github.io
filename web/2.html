Cool Cat

We use this expression to describe a person who is exceptionally fashionable and trendy.

It is also used to describe someone who is funny, charming, and quite often relaxed.

It’s thought to originate from the jazz scene.

Examples

Jethro is such a cool cat! always dresses in the latest fashion.

Yuqing is so awesome to work with. She’s funny and never gets stressed out. She’s one cool cat!

I met Tano at a jazz music performance! He plays the saxophone and he’s such a cool cat.
